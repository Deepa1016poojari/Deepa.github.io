Project

Role: Team member

Title: Automatic power factor correction.

Description: Power factor basically states how far the energy provided has been utilized.The maximum value of power factor is unity.If the power factor is near to unity, better is the utility of energy or lesser is the wastage of energy. Use of capacitor banks is a very easy method to control power factor. The development of proposed project is to enhance and upgrade the operation of capacitor banks by developing a micro-controller based control system.Whenever the load power factor is less than 0.7 or 0.8, the automatic power factor correction equipment will improve the power factor to 0.9 automatically by switching on the capacitor and display the voltage,current and power factor values on LCD.
